About Our Company

Malin Energy is an indigenous service provider driven by innovative technology in providing engineering, procurement, networking and construction services in the Nigerian energy and oil and gas industry. We are tasked with the job of reinventing engineering services and information and communication technology by streamlining development process and deploying the most advanced and proven technologies.

We strive to deliver the high quality service through strategic partnerships and relationships with local and international companies.
